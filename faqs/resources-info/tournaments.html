Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I enter a tournament?

    Players can easily join a tournament by buying in with the buy in amount. Once the tournament is live, go ahead and play your round/game and your score will automatically be recorded for you.

  • How are winners determined?

    Once the tournament has closed, the final scores in the system will be used to automatically determine final positions and payout.

  • How is the prize amount set?

    The prize amount can be set in 2 ways:

    (1) Percentage of the total pot. The pot amount is based on the number of players and the buy in amount, and will continue to grow with each entry until the tournament closes. Positions are paid out a percentage of that pot set by the tournament host. (Ex. 1st place receives 50%, 2nd place receives 30%, 3rd place receives 20%)

    (2) Fixed amount. Positions are paid out a fixed amount set by the tournament host regardless of number of entries or total funds collected from buy ins. (Ex. 1st place receives $5,000, 2nd place receives $3,000, 3rd place receives $2,000)

  • How are ties handled?

    If two or more players tie for a payout position, we combine the prize money for all tied positions and split it equally among the tied players. Example:

    The prize pool totals 100%
    1st Place gets 50%
    2nd Place gets 30%
    3rd Place gets 20%

    If two players tie for 2nd place, we combine 2nd + 3rd prize, and divide amongst the two players:
    (30%+20%)/2 = 25% each

    There's no 3rd place prize since it was absorbed in the tie.

  • How will I know if I won?

    After tournament completion you can check your finishing position on the leaderboard screen. This can be found my navigating to your profile and tapping on the card for the tournament under "My Matchups."

  • How do I collect my winnings?

    If you are in a payout position your winnings will automatically be sent to your account upon tournament completion.

  • How does replaying a tournament work?

    If replay is enabled for a tournament, you can choose to re-enter after each round ends to try and improve your score. Each replay requires a new buy-in, and you can make multiple attempts as long as the tournament is open. Only your best score from all attempts will be used for your final ranking on the leaderboard, and you'll appear just once—based on that top score. If your best score places you in a payout rank, you’ll receive one payout only, even if multiple attempts land in payout positions.

  • What if I don't see my score recorded after my game/round?

    Your score may take a few minutes to update. If you have waited and your score is still not updated please contact an employee at the facility or reach out to support@lucrasports.com

  • Can I edit or dispute my score?

    Please contact an employee at the facility right away or reach out to support@lucrasports.com

  • Can I get a refund of my buy in?

    You can only receive a refund if the tournament you joined has not started. If this is the case, please reach out to support@lucrasports.com
