transparency

Lucra is changing the game in skill-based contests disrupting the way that fans engage with their friends. Our fair and responsible platform and extensive responsible gaming policy ensures every Lucra User can play within their financial means. Lucra is committed to transparency, integrity, and fairness throughout our platform and we will always do everything possible to prevent gaming-related problems.

  • Lucra’s mission relies on transparency; at every step we give detailed information about how funds are being used on the platform.
  • Users can easily access their contest history, funds history, and fee structure
  • Fees (5%) are only taken from the contest winner, meaning you never lose more than you stake
  • Users can withdraw and deposit at a minimum of $1 to allow for complete flexibility of funds on the platform

responsible gaming

Lucra has built out an extensive Responsible Gaming policy to protect its users through a suite of on platform settings and connection to a set of external resources.

  • Manage exactly how you want to play Lucra in a healthy way through our limits on contest size, contest frequency, and deposit frequency
  • Lucra also allows users to take a break from the action with our Self-Exclusion feature, which allows users to block themselves from participating on the platform for a specific amount of time
  • Responsible Gaming is at the core of Lucra’s mission to create a transparent and fair platform where its users can feel safe to engage in fun contests with their friends
