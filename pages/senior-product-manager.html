about Lucra Sports

Lucra Sports is a social app that lets fans compete against each other for real money. The product facilitates peer-to-peer competition on the professional sports you watch (NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, NASCAR, PGA, Tennis) and 100+ recreational games you play (i.e., golf, cornhole, video games, fitness challenges, and more). Lucra allows its users to wager with their friends in a social, safe, and trackable way, creating a digital record of all your friendly wagers. Launched in October of 2021, after 18 months building a legal framework (banking, payment processing, compliance, KYC, etc.), the Lucra iOS app is currently available in over 40 states between its sports contests and recreational games. Lucra is changing the way fans engage with their friends by introducing a new social-first gaming experience.

why we are so excited:

Space: Peer-to-peer social wagering combines several attractive and under-penetrated markets. With a nascent, but evolving regulatory and legal landscape, the opportunities for us will only grow.

Stage: We have closed a successful Series A with high-profile investors in both the sports and venture capital community. We need to ramp up the team and take advantage of the early lead we have in our space.

Team: We are a team composed of repeat founders, ex-NFL Management, ex-Microsoft, ex-Goldman Sachs.

Investor Base: We are backed by some of the biggest names in sports, including NFL/NBA/MLB team owners, professional athletes and top-tier venture funds.

about you

  • You have 5-8 years of experience as a Product Manager, working directly with technical development/engineering teams
  • You’re a self-starter and are able to identify and solve problems with little guidance–proactive, bold, and resourceful ("If not me, then who?").
  • You obsess over constant product improvements and solving user problems
  • You’re skilled at balancing competing priorities and driving consensus around trade-off decisions
  • You are able to flow between thinking creatively and analytically, and between high-level strategy and detail-oriented execution
  • You have a can-do attitude and a “no task is too big or too small” mentality
  • You use product analytics, user research, and user testing to drive product strategy
  • You have strong interpersonal & communication skills, able to speak comfortably with everyone on the team from executives, to engineers, to designers, to legal & compliance leaders, to end users
  • You have experience working in a fast-paced, early-stage start-up environment and/or are incredibly enthusiastic about learning quickly in a new space.
  • You thrive on a small team where you can make a big impact. You are confident in your skills and experience, but also have the intellectual curiosity to continue learning and developing your personal and professional toolkit.
  • You can clearly articulate user needs, your decisions, and your rationale for them
  • Ideally, you have a background in working with earlier stage companies in the B2C or consumer facing world
  • You are curious about the social sports gaming space and love a little friendly competition

main responsabilites include

  • Understand and help define our product vision, continually iterating & refining it based on user insights and product analytics
  • Conduct user research and user testing to help inform the product roadmap
  • Prioritize and drive the product backlog, balancing strong technical foundations for future development, solving the most meaningful user needs, and getting solutions into users’ hands as quickly as possible to rapidly test and learn
  • Partner closely with our Scrum Master and Engineering team to meet near-term business needs and long-term opportunities
  • Work cross-functionally to take new products & features to market that measurably address user needs and/or business goals
  • Translate product direction into actionable pieces of work, with well-defined epics, user stories, and acceptance criteria
  • Build a deep understanding of our users and create innovative solutions from a place of empathy
  • Define success metrics, constantly measuring the impact of the team’s work and of our new features to facilitate data-informed decisions
