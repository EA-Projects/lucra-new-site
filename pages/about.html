OUR INVESTORS

Raptor Group

Raptor Group, led by Jim Pallotta, focuses on investing in various industries including: technology, fintech, sports, consumer, and media. Raptor Group provides unique sports-focused expertise with dozens of previous investments in the space including the Boston Celtics and AS Roma.

Seventy Six

SeventySix Capital invests in passionate and smart entrepreneurs launching game-changing startups in sports betting, esports, and sports tech. SeventySix Capital is a value-add investor for many industry peers such as Vigtory (sold to FuboTV), VSiN (sold to DraftKings), and Swish Analytics.

Victress Capital

Victress Capital is a group of investors, operators, and entrepreneurs who back bold, diverse teams that build superior consumer experiences and the technologies that power them. Victress is led by Lori Cashman and Suzanne Norris who bring extensive experience in consumer investing and an extensive network.

Marc Lasry

Marc Lasry is a co-owner of the Milwaukee Bucks and CEO and Co-Founder of Avenue Capital Group, a global investment firm with over $9 billion in assets under management.

Dennis Wong

Dennis Wong is the Vice Chairman of the LA Clippers and a board member of the NBA Board of Governors. Dennis is also the founder and CEO of Verbena Road Holdings, Ltd, an investment company with interests in commercial real estate, multifamily properties and a broad range of private investments. Prior to Verbena, Dennis co-founded SPI Holdings, LLC in 1995 to invest in real estate properties nationally. He received an MBA from Harvard Business School and an AB from Harvard College.

John Isner

John Isner is a top American professional tennis player, who has won 12 ATP titles and reached a peak ATP ranking of #8 in the world. Isner is the 2018 Miami Open Champion, has over 400 wins and $19 million earned in prize money.

Emmanuel Sanders

Emmanuel Sanders is an American football wide receiver and Super Bowl champion. He played college football at SMU, and was drafted by the Pittsburgh Steelers in the third round of the 2010 NFL Draft. Sanders won Super Bowl 50 with the Denver Broncos. He has also been a member of the San Francisco 49ers squad that made Super Bowl LIV, the New Orleans Saints, and the Buffalo Bills.

Khris Middleton

Khris Middleton is an American professional basketball player for the Milwaukee Bucks. He is a three-time NBA All-Star and won an NBA championship with the Bucks in 2021. He played college basketball for the Texas A&M Aggies from 2009 to 2012.

Julie Ertz

Julie Ertz is an American soccer player signed to the Chicago Red Stars of the National Women's Soccer League and also plays for the United States women's national soccer team. She has been part of two FIFA Women's World Cup winning teams in 2015 and 2019 and also represented US at the 2016 Olympic Games where she won gold and at the 2020 Olympic Games where she won bronze. She earned the 'U.S. Soccer Female Player of the Year' honor twice in 2017 and 2019.

Zach Ertz

Zach Ertz was originally drafted by the Eagles in the 2nd round (35th overall) of the 2013 NFL Draft. A three-time Pro Bowler who caught the game-winning TD in Super Bowl LII, Zach Ertz has produced more receptions (525) than any other NFL TE in his first seven seasons.
