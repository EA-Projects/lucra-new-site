Advisors

John Isner

John Isner is a top American professional tennis player, who has won 12 ATP titles and reached a peak ATP ranking of #8 in the world. Isner is the 2018 Miami Open Champion, has over 400 wins and $19 million earned in prize money.

Donna Orender

Donna Orender is the CEO of Orender Unlimited and the former President of the WNBA. Prior to the WNBA, Donna spent 17 years at the PGA TOUR where she was one of 3 senior executives serving in the Office of the Commissioner. Before then, she was a professional basketball player in the WBL, where she earned All-Star status.

Mike Tollin

Michael Tollin is the Co-Chairman of Mandalay Sports Media. He is an American film and television producer/director, who served as executive producer of the Emmy award-winning The Last Dance. Tollin's other career highlights include Radio, Coach Carter, Hank Aaron: Chasing the Dream, and Varsity Blues.

Jonathan Levav

Jonathan Levav is a professor of marketing at Stanford Graduate School of Business. His research is aimed at understanding consumer’s judgments and choices by using tools from experimental psychology and behavioral economics. In particular, he studies the contextual factors that influence people’s choices and judgments.
