about Lucra

Lucra is the leading technology provider of real-money gamification services. Our B2B and Direct-To-Consumer technologies facilitate real money contests on partner platforms or within their own app, available on iOS and Android. Lucra's white-label, Software Development Kit (SDK) provides partners with a comprehensive, turnkey solution to embed gamification technology directly into their platforms. This innovation offers features previously unavailable to enterprise clients, including real-money, peer-to-peer contests within their platforms. Lucra is changing the way fans engage with their friends by introducing a new, digital, social-first gaming experience.

why we are so excited:

Space: Social, real-money gamification combines several attractive and under-penetrated markets. We are first movers in this market and have an opportunity to create an industry-leading technology that can service millions of customers worldwide.

Stage: We have closed a successful Series A with high-profile, individual and institutional investors across consumer, sports, and enterprise. We need to ramp up the team and take advantage of the early lead we have in our space.

Team: We are a team composed of repeat founders from Stanford Business School, with employees from Microsoft, NASA, Goldman Sachs, AQR, Athena Health, Atlassian, and more!

Investor Base: We are backed by some of the biggest names in consumer technology and sports, including NFL/NBA/MLB/MLS/MLP team owners, professional athletes, and top-tier venture funds.

about the role

Lucra is looking for a curious, tenacious, and accountable person with a QA background to work closely with our Product, Engineering, and Support teams in an effort to create the best experience for our users. This role is a foundational one within our organization with lots of opportunity for growth as you’ll wear many hats – communicating directly with end users and partners to resolve technical issues, collaborating with product and engineering to investigate and prioritize bug reports, and working closely with our support team to handle escalated cases. This role is perfect for someone looking to gain experience in several different business functions with the opportunity to grow and specialize in any one of them.

Requirements

  • At least 1 year of QA experience.
  • Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience.
  • Primary residence in one of the following states, in which Lucra is fully operational: Alaska, Arkansas, California, Colorado, Connecticut, Florida, Georgia, Hawaii, Idaho, Illinois, Indiana, Kansas, Kentucky, Louisiana, Maine, Massachusetts, Michigan, Minnesota, Mississippi, Missouri, Nebraska, New Hampshire, New York, North Carolina, North Dakota, Oklahoma, Oregon, Pennsylvania, Rhode Island, South Carolina, Tennessee, Texas, Utah, Virginia, West Virginia, Wisconsin, Wyoming.
  • Familiarity with mobile application testing techniques (Android and iOS).
  • Fluent SQL skills.

Our ideal candidate:

  • Has experience working in a fast-paced early-stage start-up environment or is incredibly enthusiastic about learning quickly in a new space.
  • Supported releases by completing smoke and regression testing.
  • Has analyzed the impact of production issues and identified affected users and customers.
  • Has worked with the support teams, and owned bug investigations.
  • Has worked closely with the product, engineering, and customer support teams to identify improvement areas for a product.
  • Has analyzed logs and validated data using SQL queries.
  • Worked with the Product and Engineering teams to ensure a healthy, up-to-date backlog of known bug reports.
  • Participated in Root Cause Analysis and helped implement process improvements.
  • Created and monitored system health metrics.
  • Worked with the Product teams to establish scalable processes for all work streams related to new feature development, launch, and testing.
Looking for candidates for an immediate start-date.

Please email team@lucrasports.com with your resume and detailed reason as to why you want to work at Lucra (no more then one page). Salary will be based on experience.
