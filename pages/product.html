Lucra's Best In Class Risk Management Toolkit

Payment Services Integration

Our certifications and established partnerships with accredited payment service providers enables us to seamlessly process credit card and bank account transactions.

Fraud Prevention and Monitoring

Our fraud prevention and monitoring system utilizes best-practice rules, automatically blocking and suspending users who trigger any suspicious activities.

Geolocation

User location is verified during registration and whenever they engage in a contest or money-related activity to ensure compliance with local laws and regulations. This information is crucial for detecting and preventing fraudulent behavior, ensuring a safe and compliant environment for all users.

Anti-Money Laundering
(Pattern Recognition)

We have implemented stringent controls and limits to effectively mitigate and monitor risks associated with the movement of funds in and out of the platform, ensuring the utmost security and compliance.

KYC (Know Your Customer)

Prior to engaging in any contests or other money-related activities, all users undergo thorough Know Your Customer (KYC) processes. These measures are in place to verify the legal age and status of users, ensuring compliance and creating a safe environment for participation.

Monthly 3rd party reviews & annual audits

We have established a strategic partnership with a specialized third-party that focuses on financial compliance and monitoring. This independent entity conducts monthly reviews ensuring adherence to regulatory standards. We also undergo a comprehensive annual audit, and the results are submitted to our banking partners.

Transaction Monitoring

We have established a seamless integration with a renowned industry leader in fraud and anti-money laundering (AML) identification. This partnership enables us to receive real-time feedback on every financial transaction within the system, enhancing our ability to detect and prevent fraudulent activities.

Authentication

Users are securely authenticated across all partner apps and managed as unique individuals, utilizing industry-accepted mechanisms. This ensures a seamless and protected user experience, while maintaining the integrity and security of their accounts and activities.

Customer Support

We provide customer support for you and have a dedicated team answering questions straight integrated into our app.
