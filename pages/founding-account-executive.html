about Lucra

Lucra is the leading technology provider of real-money gamification services. Our B2B technology facilitates real money contests on partner platforms or within their own app, available on iOS and Android. Lucra's white-label, Software Development Kit (SDK) provides partners with a comprehensive, turnkey solution to embed gamification technology directly into their platforms. This innovation offers features previously unavailable to enterprise clients, including real-money, peer-to-peer contests within their platforms. Lucra is changing the way fans engage with their friends by introducing a new, digital, social-first gaming experience.

why we are so excited:

Space: Competitive social, real-money gamification combines several attractive and under-penetrated markets. We are first movers in the B2B distribution into this market and have an opportunity to create an industry-leading technology that can service millions of customers worldwide.

Partners: We are partnered up with some of the worlds strongest brands in friendly competition, including but not limited to: Dave & Buster’s, Touchtunes, Five Iron Golf, Hollywood.Com, RPM Raceway, The Professional Darts Corporation, and more.

Stage: We have closed a successful Series A with high-profile, top-tier institutional venture capital, family office, and HNW investors across consumer, sports, and enterprise technology. We need to ramp up the team and take advantage of the early lead we have in our space.

Team: We are a team composed of repeat founders from Stanford Business School, with employees from Microsoft, NASA, Goldman Sachs, AQR, Athena Health, Atlassian, and more!

Investor Base: We are backed by some of the biggest names in consumer technology and sports, including NFL/NBA/MLB/MLS/MLP team owners, professional athletes, and top-tier venture funds.

about the role

Lucra is looking for a curious, tenacious, and hungry individual with a SaaS sales background to lead our sales motion and strategy. This role is a two-pronged opportunity to add value in both sourcing and closing deals. The ideal candidate will help to not only expand our pipeline and conduct outreach (both cold and warm), but also take the deals through completion by selling into key stakeholders and negotiating MSAs. We are looking for a sales leader who is not afraid to roll up their sleeves and do the hard work to close a sale. Since Lucra has established itself as the first mover in a massively untapped space, this role will be sector agnostic and size agnostic, as the candidate will sell into many verticals across enterprise, mid-market, and SMB clients. This role will be a high visibility and key revenue driving position in the company, which will be reflected in the compensation. As the Founding Account Executive, the commission opportunity available within Lucra’s untapped pipeline is quite extensive. The candidate in this role will report directly to the company’s CEO, Dylan Robbins.

Requirements

  • At least 5 years+ of B2B SaaS experience.
  • Experience with, or have been exposed to larger enterprise sales ($1M+ TCV).
  • Have sold to C-suite executives, SVP, and VP level.
  • Experience creating sales motions, conducting cold and warm outreach, and negotiating B2B SaaS contracts.
  • Has experience selling a new SaaS product at a company with less than 200 employees.
  • Primary residence in the United States.

Our ideal candidate:

  • Will have demonstrated the ability to communicate, present, and influence credibly & effectively at all levels of the organization (including executive and C-suite level).
  • Has incredibly strong work ethic, hustle, and grit.
  • Doesn’t take no for an answer - but knows how to tactfully nudge and move the process along without being overbearing.
  • Is a culture carrier and brings positive energy to both their client meetings and the workplace.
  • Has professional writing and communication skills, with strong knowledge of Microsoft Suite, Google Suite, and CRM tools.
  • Has the ability to adapt to a face-paced changing business where priorities often change quickly, and new innovative products to sell are being developed at a rapid pace.
  • Is proactive and can work independently - by taking guidance from leadership, digesting and internalizing it, and putting those learnings into action on their own without much hand holding.
  • Has the drive to win, is not just looking to meet their quota but blast through it to both help the company succeed but also drive monetary success for themselves.
Looking for candidates for an immediate start-date.

Please email team@lucrasports.com with your resume and detailed reason as to why you want to work at Lucra (no more then one page). Resumes without a cover letter describing specifically why you are a fit for the role based on the requirements above will not be considered. Salary will be based on experience level, commission structure and quotas will be shared during later stages of the interview process.
