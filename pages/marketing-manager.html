about Lucra Sports

Lucra Sports is a peer-to-peer sports gaming platform that sits uniquely at the intersection of sports betting and fantasy sports. Lucra allows users to create player vs. player contests in which they compete against friends for real money. Social features are the heart of Lucra and allow you to share contests and smack talk with friends and fans. Backed by Marc Lasry of the Milwaukee Bucks, Zach and Julie Ertz, and John Insner among other top investors, Lucra is ready to take the sports wagering industry by storm. After closing our Series A funding in April 2022, it is time to ramp up the team and take advantage of the early lead we have in our space. Get in the game!

  • Free merch and free play on the Lucra app

  • Consideration for internship/full-time position
what you’ll do
  • Host events with your friends and campus community

our ideal candidate
  • Diverse and well-rounded with the ability to connect with varied groups of people
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Charismatic, energetic, and outgoing personality
  • Immersed in student life including clubs and organizations
  • Sophomore, junior, or senior working towards a bachelor's degree
explanation of role

Lucra Sports Campus Brand Ambassador will be responsible for driving user adoption, hosting sponsored events, and soliciting customer feedback, amongst other aspects related to the promotion of the Lucra brand. This candidate will gain valuable experience in the areas of marketing, partnerships, budgeting, and user activations.

Candidates will be paired with other ambassadors on their campuses to ensure successful program management. The highest quality candidates will be selected to oversee several schools and regions programs.

This is a seasonal position for currently enrolled graduate and undergraduate students only. This position will run from September to November 2022, with potential to renew for Jan-May 2023.
