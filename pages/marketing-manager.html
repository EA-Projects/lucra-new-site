about Lucra Sports

Lucra Sports is a peer-to-peer sports gaming platform that sits uniquely at the intersection of sports betting and fantasy sports. Lucra allows users to create player vs. player contests in which they compete against friends for real money. Customization of players and stats allows users to be more creative (including cross-sport contests) than existing options in the marketplace. Social features are the heart of Lucra and allow you to share contests and smack talk with friends and fans. Lucra is founded upon transparency, with fees lower than any sportsbook and an easy-to-use platform that users can trust.

After closing Series A funding in April 2022, it is time to ramp up the team and take advantage of the early lead we have in our space. Get in the game!

explanation of role

Lucra Sports Marketing Managers will be responsible for driving user adoption, hosting sponsored events, and soliciting customer feedback, amongst other aspects related to the promotion of the Lucra brand. This candidate will gain valuable experience in the areas of marketing, partnerships, budgeting, and user activations.

Candidates will be paired with other ambassadors on their campuses to ensure successful program management. The highest quality candidates will be selected to oversee several schools and regions programs.

This position will run from September to November 2022, with the potential to renew (and get promoted) for Jan-May 2023 depending on performance. The candidate will work with various teams across Lucra (marketing, strategy, product) and report to Lucra’s Business Development Manager, Raebekkah Pratt-Clarke.
