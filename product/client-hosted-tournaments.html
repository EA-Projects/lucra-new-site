Client-Hosted Tournaments

Drive Traffic & (Re)visitation With Large Scale Tournaments & Events

Lucra’s Client-Hosted Tournaments product empowers your brand to host their own free-to-play or real-money large-scale tournaments or events—directly inside your platform. Ideal for entertainment venues, events, or social-based apps, it drives repeat usage and creates organic, community-driven engagement.
