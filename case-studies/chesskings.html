About Chess Kings

Chess Kings aims to be the first Media, Entertainment, Fantasy and Gaming platform for chess players.

With a vision to reach the estimated 600 million chess players globally and connect with the world's 2,000 Grandmasters, Chess Kings will offer the most innovative fantasy experience in all of sports by allowing novice players to play against the pros. The Chess Kings platforms will revolutionise how fans engage with the world's most intellectually stimulating game.
