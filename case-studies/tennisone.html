About TennisONE

The TennisONE App is the award-winning global app for all things tennis, engaging ~1M users with live scores, highlights, streaming, content, and now, social gaming. The TennisONE App has served as the “Official App” of dozens of global ATP and WTA tournaments, including the majority of the U.S. Open Series and other prestigious events. The TennisONE App’s CrowdLIVE Interactive platform was named a 2023 “Streaming Product of the Year” by the National Association of Broadcasters.
