About CliqFit

CliqFit is the only fitness app that lets users compete in games powered entirely by their workouts, and this is just the beginning.

CliqFit offers a suite of fitness games, including Bingo, FitSix, and dynamic step challenges, which expand the ways people can challenge each other, stay motivated, and make fitness fun. With an emphasis on inclusivity and enjoyment, CliqFit helps people at all fitness levels connect through play while achieving their activity goals. For more information, visit cliqfitapp.com and download the CliqFit app today.
